ChatShare — AI chats you can learn from, laugh with, and share

Bring your conversations to life with ChatShare. Talk with friendly AI for learning, creativity, and fun — then publish the best exchanges as public links to major AI platforms. Save the links you love and revisit them anytime.

Key features
- Natural AI conversations: Chat with helpful, conversational AI that explains concepts, brainstorms ideas, and keeps things entertaining.
- Share public links: Publish any chat as a public link that can be opened in ChatGPT, Claude (Code), or Microsoft Copilot public links so others can view or continue the conversation.
- Favorites & collections: Bookmark and organize your favourite chat links for quick access and future reference.
- Lightweight and fast: Clean UI focused on chats and sharing, optimized for quick browsing and reading.

Why you'll love ChatShare
- Learn faster — turn explanations into shareable examples you can revisit or send to friends and colleagues.
- Collaborate easily — publish a chat link and let others pick up the conversation in their preferred AI workspace.
- Keep inspiration handy — save creative answers, step-by-step guides, or helpful code snippets as favorites.

How it works
1. Start a conversation with our AI assistant and explore ideas, questions, or code.
2. Tap "Share" on any chat to generate a public link.
3. Open the link in supported services (ChatGPT, Claude, Copilot) or share it directly.
4. Save important links to your favorites for later.

Privacy & safety
We respect your privacy. You control which chats are published — only chats you choose to publish become public links. For details, see our Privacy Policy at https://chatshare.dev/policy.
